About SIFF

Singles in Faith Fellowship (SIFF) is a Christ-centred community dedicated to helping people become who God has called them to be through biblical teaching, intentional formation and authentic community.

Grounded in 2 Chronicles 27:6, SIFF is built on the conviction that lasting relationships are not created by chance, but through intentional preparation before the Lord. We believe that becoming the person God has called you to be is the foundation for healthy relationships, purposeful living and lasting impact.

Through biblical teaching, honest conversations and practical application, SIFF provides a safe space where people can grow spiritually, emotionally, mentally and relationally while building authentic community with others on a similar journey.

Our community is built around three areas of formation.

Personal Formation

Developing spiritual maturity, emotional health, self-awareness, wisdom, resilience and Christ-like character, while building the practical life skills needed to flourish in every season.

Healthy Relationships

Learning biblical principles for building strong friendships, setting healthy boundaries, communicating effectively, resolving conflict and cultivating relationships that honour God.

Kingdom-Centred Partnerships

Preparing for and building marriages that reflect God’s design through biblical understanding, intentional preparation and practical wisdom.

Whether you are single, dating, engaged or married, SIFF exists to help people become who God has called them to be through biblical truth, intentional formation and authentic community.
